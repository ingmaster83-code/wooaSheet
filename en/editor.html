FAQ

What formulas are supported?

Powered by HyperFormula, supporting 380+ functions including SUM, AVERAGE, IF, VLOOKUP, INDEX/MATCH, COUNT, MAX, and MIN.

Is there a file size limit?

Up to 10MB is supported. Very large files may be slow depending on your browser's performance.

Is my data stored on a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any server.
