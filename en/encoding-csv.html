FAQ

Why does Excel show garbled Korean characters?

If a CSV is saved as UTF-8 without a BOM marker, Excel may misread it. Converting to UTF-8 here automatically adds the BOM so Excel reads it correctly.

Can auto-detection be wrong?

Yes — short files or files with only ASCII characters may be detected incorrectly. Manually select the source encoding in that case.

Still garbled after conversion?

Try a different source encoding. If no encoding works, the file itself may be corrupted.
