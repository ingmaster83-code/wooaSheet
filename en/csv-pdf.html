💡 Tips

  • Column widths are distributed automatically based on content length. Very long values are truncated with an ellipsis (…)
  • For tables with many columns, choose "Landscape" for more horizontal space
  • The first PDF generation downloads and embeds the font, so it may take a few seconds

FAQ

What happens if there are too many rows for one page?

The table automatically continues onto the next page, and the header row is repeated at the top of every page.

Will non-Latin characters render correctly?

Yes. A Unicode font is embedded directly into the PDF, so text displays correctly on any device.

Are my files uploaded to the internet?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser and files are never sent to a server.
