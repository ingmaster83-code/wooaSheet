FAQ

Will special characters display correctly?

Yes. UTF-8 BOM is automatically added during CSV conversion so special characters display correctly in Excel.

What about multi-sheet Excel files?

Only the first sheet is converted to CSV. You can open other sheets using the Spreadsheet Editor.

Is my file sent to a server?

No. All conversion is processed in your browser. Your file is never sent externally.
