💡 Tips

  • Dates in YYYY-MM-DD format sort correctly using Text sort
  • For columns with numbers, choose "Number" sort to order 1, 2, 10 correctly
  • Empty cells are always placed at the end after sorting

FAQ

Can I sort by multiple columns?

Currently only single-column sorting is supported. Use the Spreadsheet Editor for multi-column sort.

Is the header row sorted too?

No. The first row is treated as a header and always stays at the top.

Is my file uploaded to a server?

No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
