FAQ

What file formats are supported?

CSV, XLSX, and XLS files are supported. Maximum file size is 10MB.

How do I save the chart?

Click "Save as PNG" to download a high-resolution PNG image of your chart.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. All processing happens entirely in your browser. Your files are never sent to any external server.
